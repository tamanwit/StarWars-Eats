About Us




Star Wars is a space opera that takes place "a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away". The series follows Luke Skywalker, a young man who becomes involved in a war between an authoritarian empire and rebel forces. Luke, along with Han Solo and Princess Leia, must save Leia from captivity on a space station commanded by Darth Vader.

The Star Wars series is known for its advancements in special effects, including imaginative alien creatures, mechanical droids, and elaborate space battles. The opening crawl, which scrolls up and away from the bottom of the screen, was inspired by the opening crawls of the original Flash Gordon and Buck Rogers film serials. The crawls on the original trilogy films were created by filming physical models laid out on the floor
